Maintain Social Distancing This is the most important step of all. Social distancing or self-isolation is extremely essential during these critical days. You can achieve this by avoiding contact with people who shows symptoms of COVID 19, or anyone who is coughing or sneezing. Work from home whenever and wherever possible. Avoid using public transport if you absolutely need to step outdoors. Avoid any kinds of gatherings or crowded places like malls and local markets. Most importantly, stay at home and tell your peers to do the same. Break the chain.
Wash Your Hands This step is very crucial whether or not you have been outdoors. Keep yourself clean and maintain hygiene standards at all times. No, sanitizers are not your only way out to be germ-free. Do not underestimate the power of handwashes (your floral handwashes won't save you). The age-old handwashes from Dettol and Lifebuoy are your only saviour now. Not only your hands, clean yourselves regularly with hygienic soaps.
Keep Yourself Updated It is very crucial during this global epidemic to stay updated and to keep an eye on the local news. Stay informed about the steps that your government is taking to cope with this epidemic and as a responsible citizen, value them. Do not trust sources from Facebook or other Social Media or blogs that have not been verified. Avoid fake news and misleading information.

Exercise Besides work from home also try workout from home. Exercises improve your immune system and help your body fight infections. It has great effects on your body's metabolic activities.
