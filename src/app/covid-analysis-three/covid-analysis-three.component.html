Motivation In the representations given below we have tried to put forth the cumulative and the daily stats of the states that top in the confirmed, recovered and deceased counts respectively. We have tried to analyse the performance of the states for each given date and under all the three parameters of confirmed, recovered and deceased cases due to COVID-19 as per latest date (21 / 04 / 2020).
